Privacy Policy for Twitch Austrian Dialect Translator

Last updated: December 2024

DATA COLLECTION
This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. 

LOCAL STORAGE
The extension stores only user preferences (toggle settings) locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

PERMISSIONS USED
- Host permission (twitch.tv): Required to read and modify chat text for translation
- Storage permission: Required to save your preference settings locally

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
This extension does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. All translation processing occurs locally in your browser.
